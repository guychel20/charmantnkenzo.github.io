# Professional and Spiritual Alignment

My professional skills and spiritual beliefs are closely connected.

## Technical Skills and Agency
In IT, problem solving requires initiative. Doctrine and Covenants 58:26–28 teaches that individuals must act and not wait to be commanded. This directly applies to troubleshooting systems and improving processes.

## Security and Integrity
Cybersecurity requires honesty, responsibility, and accountability. Protecting user data is not just a technical task but a moral obligation.

## Collaboration and Service
Working in teams requires patience, communication, and understanding. Principles like “seek first to understand” help build stronger collaboration and better results.

## Continuous Improvement
Technology constantly evolves, requiring ongoing learning. This aligns with spiritual growth, where individuals are encouraged to continually improve and develop.

## Conclusion
My goal is to become a professional who not only solves technical problems but also reflects integrity, service, and leadership in all aspects of my work.
