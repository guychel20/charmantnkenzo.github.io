# About Me

My name is Charmant Nkenzo. I am an IT professional and Technology Coordinator committed to integrating technical skills with principled leadership. My academic and professional experience at Ensign College and BYU-Pathway has taught me that technology is most effective when directed by purpose, ethics, and service.

My guiding principles are:

1. Gather Together in One All Things in Christ
2. Single-Eyed Devotion and Ethical Stewardship
3. Covenant-Centered Self-Reliance and Service

These principles shape how I approach systems, leadership, service, and learning.
